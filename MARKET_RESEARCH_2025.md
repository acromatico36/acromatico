### **DON'T Show**: "$6,000" upfront
### **DO Show**: "3 easy payments of $2,000"

**Psychology**: Breaking down large purchases reduces sticker shock and increases conversion

1. **First payment (today)**: 33% - Secures date, non-refundable retainer
2. **Second payment**: 33% - Due at midpoint between booking and wedding
