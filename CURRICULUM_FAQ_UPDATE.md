#### **Manual Mode Mastery Section**
**Why Manual Mode Matters:**
- Complete creative control over every shot
- Master the **Exposure Triangle**:
  - **Aperture (f-stop)**: Controls depth of field
    - Wide open (f/1.8) = blurry background (portraits)
    - Closed down (f/16) = everything sharp (landscapes)
  - **Shutter Speed**: Controls motion
    - Fast (1/1000s) = freeze action
    - Slow (1/30s) = motion blur (waterfalls, light trails)
  - **ISO**: Controls light sensitivity
    - Low (100-400) = clean, sharp images
    - High (1600+) = grainy but bright in darkness
