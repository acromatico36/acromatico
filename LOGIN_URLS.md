**Solutions:**
1. ✅ **Use Option 1** (quick-login.html) - it bypasses all cache
2. ✅ **Clear browser cache**: Cmd+Shift+Delete (iPhone Safari: Settings > Safari > Clear History)
3. ✅ **Force refresh**: Cmd+Shift+R (desktop) or hard reload
4. ✅ **Use incognito/private mode**

### Problem: Page won't load or shows blank screen

**Solutions:**
1. Check your internet connection
2. Try a different browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari)
3. Disable any ad blockers or privacy extensions
4. Copy the URL exactly as shown above
