## ✅ NEW FAQ QUESTION ADDED

### **"What if we join mid-year?"**

**Answer:**

**No problem!** You can join anytime during the school year.

**For Monthly Plans:**
- You'll start with the current month's curriculum and continue through June
- Any months you missed will roll over to the next school year
- Your child completes the full 10-month journey

**For Annual Plans:**
- If you join mid-year (e.g., January), your 10-month annual plan covers:
  - January-June (current school year)
  - September-December (next school year)
- You get the full curriculum and 20% savings, just spread across two school years

- **Year 2 (Sept-Dec 2026)**: Months 7-10 (4 months)
- **Total**: 10 months of instruction
- **Savings**: 20% off monthly pricing across the entire 10 months
