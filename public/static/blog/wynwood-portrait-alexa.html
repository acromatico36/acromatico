Wynwood Portrait | Alexa

November 30, 2021 • Wynwood

Had the pleasure of working with Alexa for this amazing Wynwood portrait featuring some of the amazing boho accessories from Kashii Designs!

Everyone knows Wynwood screams color and these amazing colors brought to life these beautiful crafted pieces made by wayuu artisans right from Colombia.

We got to shoot some of these pieces to be featured on their website and ig to prove that these amazing accessories are not just for boho beachwear but you can also have fun, feel beautiful and wear as a casual going out piece all at the same time. Such versatile wear and we can’t wait to get our hands on them!
