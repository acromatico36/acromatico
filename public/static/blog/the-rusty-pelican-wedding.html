The Rusty Pelican Wedding

Photography • January 22, 2019

Every time we get to do a wedding at the Rusty Pelican in Key Biscayne is such treat! The views of the Miami skyline are breathtaking and the food is incredibly amazing. You can’t go wrong with The Rusty Pelican as your venue, one of our favorites for sure!  Kelly and Norberto definitely have a very unique taste and everything they envisioned for their wedding day their amazing planner Gabriela Sousa Events made it happen effortlessly, highly recommend her!
