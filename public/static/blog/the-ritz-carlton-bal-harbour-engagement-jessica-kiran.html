The Ritz-Carlton Bal Harbour Engagement | Jessica + Kiran

April 24, 2021 • Miami

We always LOVE it when we do a session at the Ritz Carlton in Bal Harbour! We used to be next door neighbors with this beautiful hotel a few years ago before babies and it bring back so many beautiful memories!

The original plan was to take the pictures at the beautiful beach they have in front but a tornado warning came earlier and the strong winds and uncontrollable rain made this impossible. What do you do when your clients fly all the way to Miami just to have their photos done with you? You make them happen no matter what!

So we we had to quickly adapt and improvise and ended up taking them inside the hotel. This place is so beautiful that it easily became a good trade! Enjoy some of our favorites from this day and this lovely couple. Jessica and Kiran, you guys were amazing and we can’t wait till your wedding day:
