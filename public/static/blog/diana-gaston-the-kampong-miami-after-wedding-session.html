Diana + Gaston | The Kampong Miami After Wedding Session

November 13, 2015 • Miami

Meet Diana and Gaston: the happily ever after. We had the awesome opportunity to shoot these guys only days after their very intimate wedding. They chose to do their after session at the beautiful Kampong gardens. Despite risking falling in the pool, running into some spiderwebs and being attacked by mosquitoes, this session was a hit! Typically you’re fortunate to wear your wedding attire once or twice, these guys got to wear it three times in a row since.
