Miami Family Session | Von Kretschmann Family

Photography • January 22, 2022

All the way from Chile meet this gorgeous family! Carol (the one with the green shirt) is my sister in law that I happen to consider my best friend in life. I know not everyone is so blessed to have friendships like this one so if you do, always try to remind them how special they are. It’s funny how things happened, how we met and all the things we’ve lived together ever since. We’ve known each other since we were 13, we even went to the same school and we’ve been besties and sisters ever since. So having the opportunity to photograph her family visiting from Chile was a special treat for myself as I love them all so much!

For this session we decided to take a nice afternoon stroll at the park. We didn’t have enough time before the sun left us so we ended up shooting the last images at my their home. We laughed so much and had a great time just like the beautiful old days.
