Kelly + Kurt | Hilton Hotel Fort Lauderdale Wedding

Photography • February 17, 2016

This past weekend, we had the chance to shoot in one of our favorite Caribbean Islands, the Bahamas! Wait, JUST KIDDING! But it sure did feel like it with the crystal clear turquoise ocean waters. Kelly and Kurt got ready at the Hilton Resort in Fort Lauderdale Beach, where they later celebrated their wedding in a gorgeous environment. The day was perfect, the air was crisp, waters bluer than ever, and this bride shined like no other day. We captured a few emotional moments where we literally had to hold our tears in. You might remember them from their engagement session last spring. It was so great working with this couple once again.  Congrats again guys, it was an honor being part of your big day!
