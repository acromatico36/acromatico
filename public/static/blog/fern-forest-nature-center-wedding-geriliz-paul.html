Fern Forest Nature Center Wedding | Geriliz + Paul

Photography • October 4, 2021

Limoncello, San Pellegrino, Pasta and ferns. This is how I’d sum up this Italian inspired Floridian wedding at the Fern Forest Nature Center.

It was such a pleasure finally getting to meet Geriliz and Paul. I have to say, this has been one of the most planned out weddings that actually everything went exactly as planned (and you KNOW that’s rare). You’re officially hired as a wedding planner, hehe!

Despite having a small, intimate wedding, this was definitely one to remember. The decor was very Capri inspired and everything was absolutely beautiful.

Side note: what I do love about smaller weddings is that you can forego all the other details and focus on the couple. You can spend a lot more time together and it’s an absolute photographer’s dream to be able to have plenty of time with the couple.
