Warwick, New York Wedding | Jemma + Luke

October 1, 2019 • New York

This next destination wedding took place in Warwick, NY. You might remember Jemma and Luke from their engagement sesh last winter. It’s crazy to believe how fast time flew and these dear friends of ours are now married!

They had thee most gorgeous end of summer wedding of all time. It was the perfect day for an outdoor wedding at the Blue Arrow Farm. This place was full of character and so many adventures. Jemma and Luke said their “I Do’s” and the bees would just not “let them bee”. This thug of a bride got her finger stung during the ceremony and she literally kept going like a true champ. I nearly had a heart attack as I tried to get a bee untangled from my hair but, besides that, this wedding was seriously perfect and one of a kind.
