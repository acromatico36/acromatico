Awosting Falls Engagement | Nessa + Torry

Photography • December 23, 2021

They’ve been friends since they were 17. He loves Brooklyn, she loves nature. Meet Nessa and Torry!

He popped the question right at Central Park, I mean, how dreamy is that? Despite both being from the city, they wanted an upstate engagement session. Tricky part? Um, it’s kind of freezing!

Despite the ridiculously cold weather, we decided to go for it. Awosting Falls is the perfect spot for an engagement session. The best part? It was totally empty! The cold kept everyone away and the only brave souls to get anywhere near close to waterfalls was us.

Let me tell you: Nessa and Torry made this session a breeze. They made 23 degrees look like a warm, sunny, crisp fall day. My fingers literally almost fell off. Face was almost paralizing (jk! but seriously, it was cold!) and I came totally unprepared.

I would do it all over again just to shoot these two. I mean, check out these warm, moody, feels and some of my favorites from this Awosting Falls engagement sesh. Can’t wait to do it all over again in April for their wedding. So excited!

Congrats on your engagement and upcoming wedding!
