Melina + Kyle | Bridal Shower

Photography • August 3, 2010

Melina’s aunt hired us to cover this amazing bridal shower as a surprise for her. To our surprise, we knew Kyle from before. Small world! It was an Italian-themed bridal shower, maybe the most detailed one we’ve ever assisted. Everything was beautiful, from the decorations to the food to the gourgeous views of the golf courses. Even from his Italian background, Italo approved the food! Yummy! Enjoy the pictures and congratulations to the future bride and groom. You make a beautiful couple!
