Brooklyn New York Engagement | Geeta + Vir

July 2, 2020 • New York, Brooklyn

“We were supposed to get married last month in Mexico, 300 people”. Not an unusual quote. So many people have had to change their wedding plans due to covid, Geeta and Vir were no exception.

Despite it all, these guys are making the BEST of the situation to still move forward with their wedding plans, this time, in New York!

It was so fun and challenging at the same time shooting with a mask on and trying to guide them and having to yell so they could hear me. Literally: challenge accepted. Thanks guys for being SO patient and accommodating!

These cuties chose Brooklyn for their engagement session. We came nice and early to the Brooklyn Bridge where every runner, walker and New Yorker that passed by just congratulated these two! These supermodels rocked their engagement session and I just can’t wait to shoot their Indian Wedding at the end of the month!

Brooklyn is a special place for them, this is where they celebrated their “supposed-to-be wedding day” and also where they will be spending their happily ever after.
