Monica + Camilo | Carolina Golf Club Wedding

Photography • December 3, 2013

This last wedding was very true to it’s roots, we felt like we were taken right to Colombia! We had an awesome time shooting Monica and Camilo’s e-session not so long ago. We took their getting ready shots at the beautiful Coral Springs Marriott Golf Club. The whole family was there, literally…even Monica and Camilo’s baby…that is, their 4 month puppy. What a cutie! Their wedding reception was held at the Carolina Golf Club in Margate, what a gorgeous place! This wedding was very relaxing and fun, so happy to be sharing some of our favorites from that day. Stay tuned for their after wedding session coming up next! Enjoy =) :
