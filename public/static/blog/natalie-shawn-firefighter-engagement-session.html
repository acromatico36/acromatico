Natalie + Shawn | Firefighter Engagement Session

Photography • October 21, 2015

Keep calm and let me save your kitty. That’s how Shawn won over Natalie’s heart. Too good to be true right? He might not have saved her kitty, but he is a firefighter and has her heart! It’s not too often we get to work with these amazing hard workers and their cool trucks. Well, this time, we did! Not only that, but we got to work with an old friend of ours that we’ve known since we were kids. Natalie and Shawn were such awesome models and we had an amazing time shooting them in Markham Park.
