Glen Island Park Maternity, NY | Paula

Photography • March 30, 2022

For this next maternity sesh, we ended up nailing the PERFECT day during this bipolar spring weather here in NY.

Glen Island Park is such a great, diverse, location for some awesome pics. From castle to ruins, harbor to docks, greenery to beautiful trees. It has it all! On this 70 degree day, we took some light and airy boho shots of this mommy to be, Paula.

Maternity sessions, to me, aren’t just about the belly but the journey. What you’re feeling, how empowered you are during this process, the things your body is capable of: hosting a little human right in your belly.

They’ll be expecting a sweet baby girl in just a few days and we can’t wait to meet her! Stay tuned for the baked product, aka, the baby. HEHE!

Congrats again guys on this new journey! Can’t wait to meet her <3
