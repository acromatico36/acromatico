Noris + Ramon | Miami Beach After Wedding Session

Photography • June 21, 2010

Noris and Ramon have been married for over a year now, and they weren’t quite ready to put away their wedding attire, at least not until now. Noris was totally up to trash the dress in Miami Beach! And not only did they want an after wedding session, but also a portrait session to go with it in Miami’s Design District. It was so much fun and refreshing to get in the water and stroll through the city. Enjoy!
