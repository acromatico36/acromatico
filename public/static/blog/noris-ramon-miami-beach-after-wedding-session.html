Noris + Ramon | Miami Beach After Wedding Session

June 21, 2010 • Miami

Noris and Ramon have been married for over a year now, and they weren’t quite ready to put away their wedding attire, at least not until now. Noris was totally up to trash the dress in Miami Beach!
