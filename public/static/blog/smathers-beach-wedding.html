Smathers Beach: The Perfect Wedding Destination

When it comes to saying “I do”, there is no place more magical than Smathers Beach in Key West, Florida. With its sugar-white sand and turquoise waters, Smathers Beach is the perfect setting for a romantic beach wedding.

Located just a short drive from Key West, Smathers Beach is easily accessible for all your guests. And with its convenient location, there is no need to worry about guests getting lost or running late.

If you are looking for a beach wedding destination that is truly unique and unforgettable, then look no further than Smathers Beach in Key West, Florida!

Why Smathers Beach is the perfect wedding destination

Smathers Beach is the perfect destination for your beach wedding for a number of reasons.

First, the beach is absolutely beautiful and is sure to provide a stunning backdrop for your wedding photos.

Second, the beach is conveniently located and is easily accessible for all your guests. And finally, the beach is a unique and unforgettable setting for your wedding day.

Make your wedding day unforgettable at Smathers Beach

There are a number of ways to make your wedding day at Smathers Beach truly unforgettable. A stunning wedding dress will surely compliment the beach setting and make you stand out.

Opting in for a beach-themed wedding cake is sure to impress your guests. And planning to say your “I do’s” at sunset will make a truly romantic and unforgettable experience.

Casa Marina – an incredible Key West venue for your wedding

If you are looking for an incredible Key West venue for your beach wedding, look no further than Casa Marina. Casa Marina is a historic oceanfront resort that offers a variety of unique and beautiful event spaces for your wedding.

From the stunning beachfront lawn to the elegant ballroom, Casa Marina is sure to have a space that is perfect for your wedding. If you are searching for a true beachside wedding venue, look no further than the intimate Casa Marina beach. This unique spot is perfect for a small wedding or elopement and offers beautiful views of the water. The charming Pavilion provides an ideal place to exchange your vows, with its scenic views of the sand and sea.

For something truly unique, check out the stunning Casa Marina Courtyard. This romantic setting is complete with oversized hanging lanterns and bubbling fountains that create a one-of-a-kind atmosphere for your big day. The gorgeous courtyard also provides plenty of room for dining and dancing – making it perfect for an outdoor reception under the stars.

Casa Marina also features a variety of indoor event spaces to suit every taste. The elegant Key West Ballroom has high ceilings and beautiful chandeliers, while the oceanfront banquet room boasts stunning views of the water. If you are looking for a slightly more laid-back atmosphere, try holding your wedding in the historic Library Bar or on the spacious Outdoor Terrace.

Key west is truly the ultimate South Florida destination to get married

When It comes to weddings, Key west is truly the ultimate South Florida destination to get married. Why? The weather is beautiful all year long, the island vibe and crystal-clear waters offer a truly unique setting, and there are tons of great venues to choose from.

Whether you’re looking for a beachfront ceremony or a grand ballroom affair, you can find it in Key West. And with so many things to do on the island, your guests will be extremely entertained, and feel like they’re on vacation while having so much fun during your most special day!
