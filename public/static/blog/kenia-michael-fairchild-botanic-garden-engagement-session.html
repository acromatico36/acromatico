Kenia + Michael | Fairchild Botanic Garden Engagement Session

Photography • August 6, 2014

The greatest thing about summer in South Florida has got to be the rain….NOT! Kenia and Michael, the highschool sweethearts, had to postpone their session due to the crazy rain that has been coming our way. Despite the storms,  postponing, and heat,  their engagement session was clear from ugly clouds and raindrops! The day couldn’t have been more gorgeous where they chose to do their session at the beautiful Fairchild Botanical Gardens. It was so much fun shooting these two! Not only were they high school sweethearts, but they also are currently going through their college years together in the same location.
