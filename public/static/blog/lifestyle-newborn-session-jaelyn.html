Lifestyle Newborn Session | Jaelyn

March 30, 2022

This lifestyle newborn session was extremely special to me. I got to photograph my dear friends’ newborn, Jaelyn. They’re not just any friends, they’re the couple that we got to see fall in love with eachother right from the very day they met. I photographed their proposal, engagement, wedding, baby reveal and now this newborn session.

I’ve never seen so much love in one room. Being able to capture some natural, candid, shots of this new mama was just an amazing experience. The bond all three of them have in such a short time was so beautiful.

Between a few breast feeds, sing alongs, laughs, pees, a couple diaper changes later..here you have him: Jaelyn Zion in PERSON (or photos for now..until you get to meet this amazing, little human being).

Jaelyn, I already love you. Thank you for having two amazing humans that already love you so so much. This world is a handful but your world will be filled with so much love and people that love, protect and take care of you.

Welcome to the world! I think you should let me take your wedding pics too. I hope I still “have it” by then :’)
