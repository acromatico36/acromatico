The Scovino Family | Key Biscayne Bill Baggs Family Session

April 9, 2012

I (Ale) used to work with Kathy and Francisco back when my photography was just my side job. I remember that we’ve been planning to do a family session for that long! We finally did it! It was so much fun to work with all of them, they made it so easy for us and we just fell in love with Francesca and Valentina, they were always laughing and playing around. It felt like we were just shooting a Sunday family fun day instead of working. You guys have a beautiful family! Thanks for the good times 😉
