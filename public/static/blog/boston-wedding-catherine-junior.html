Boston Wedding | Catherine + Junior

June 28, 2019

Dahling we’re in Bawston! Yes, this next wedding took place In Massachusetts!

You might remember Catherine and Junior from their fabulous engagement session in Boston. We met these two cuties at our friends/client’s wedding and now we all get to be together in one room celebrating this very special day.

Truthfully the weather couldn’t have been more gorgeous. It was specifically sunny despite the crazy summer up and downs we’ve had in the North East.

This was such a touching wedding with plenty of laughs and tears of joy. Catherine and Junior said their “I do’s” and celebrated with their family and friends at the Highfields Golf & Country Club. Had such a fun time shooting this wedding.Thank you guys so much for letting us be a huge part of it and for having us. We had such a blast!
