Spencer and Sequoia | Arts Garage Concert

December 11, 2017

So, after such a long wait, the day is finally here: we go to see Spencer and Sequoia…LIVE!

We had been following them for a while on social media and instantly fell in love with this unforgettable and talented duo: Spencer and Sequoia. So you can imagine how excited we were to actually get to hear them in person They are one of the most down to earth people we’ve ever met. Spencer and Sequoia were SOLD OUT at this full-house show and with every reason to be! Their unique voices filled the room with so much love and energy. We were so excited to be part of their amazing show at the Arts Garage in Delray Beach.

It was so lovely hearing their love story and how it was “love at first sound” rather than the usual cliché of love at first sight. These cuties fell in love with each others music and song writing. They told their life story through music and it was such a heart melting, fun show to watch.

Spencer and Sequoia had the talented Drew Tucker (itsnotaxylophone but a vibraphone) and Claudens Louis (all about the sax, the sax, the sax…ophone!) joining them performing in this amazing band. Their vibes and symphony all together was just out of this world! These guys all have true passion for music and this is their life. You literally had to be there to see them rock this show!
