Jaclyn + Jared | Miami Beach Portrait Session

September 16, 2014 • Miami, South Florida

Coming all the way from Missouri, Jaclyn and Jared decided to make their trip to Florida memorable experience. How? By capturing a little bit of their Miami experience with a beautiful portrait session. They chose to work with our amazingly talented in-house photographers Vanessa & Tica and they rocked this session!  They really enjoyed shooting in the beaches of Miami, the heart of South Florida. This lovely in-love couple was just way too cute. We hope you guys get to relive your weekend getaway with this little preview. Come back to Florida soon!
