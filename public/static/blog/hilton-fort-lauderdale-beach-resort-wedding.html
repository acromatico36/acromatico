HILTON FORT LAUDERDALE BEACH RESORT WEDDING | VERONICA + JAMES

Photography • May 14, 2019

It’s not often you get to find your perfect match. But when you do, you never let go of that special person, because you know you’ve got too much to win and too much to lose. You may remember Veronica and James from the fort lauderdale engagement session we photographed last year at the Anglins Pier. It was a very special time because we got to photograph these two lovebirds behind the scenes. This past weekend, they finally tied the knot, and they had an amazing Hilton Fort Lauderdale Beach Resort wedding. This is what happens when a Venezuelan crowd mixes with an American crowd – pure fun! Enjoy some of our favorites, the video is coming soon, stay tuned!
