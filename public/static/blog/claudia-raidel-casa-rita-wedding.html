Claudia + Raidel | Casa Rita Wedding

March 8, 2011 • Davie

We had a great time taking pictures of Claudia and Raidel’s wedding. Here are some of our favorites! The wedding was held at the Casa Rita mansion in Davie, FL.  It was a very intimate wedding, with an international mixture of family from Tenerife (Spain) and the best of Cuba. We went right after the ceremony to take pictures at the Tree Tops park, and ended the night with some good dancing and partying. As you may have guessed, it was definitely non-stop dancing! Enjoy!
