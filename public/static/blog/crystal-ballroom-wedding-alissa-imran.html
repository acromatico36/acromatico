Crystal Ballroom Wedding | Alissa + Imran

December 4, 2021 • Fort Lauderdale

We had the opportunity to shoot Alissa and Imran’s Indian wedding ceremony and now we get to do it all over again at the Crystal Ballroom in Beach Place, Fort Lauderdale!

This multicultural couple has been a true blast to work with! A little boy, a newborn and having to plan two weddings, sure wasn’t an easy task. Yet, they totally nailed it and we’re so happy to have been a part of your special days.

This non-traditional couple and the amazing team deserves a huge shot out, once again, for making it all happen:

MUA: Deja Lanee + Make Me Up Nyrva

Hair: @lisasmakeover

Tassa Band: Dynamic Rhythms Junior

Venue: Crystal Ballroom Beach Place

Hotel: Marriott Beach Place

Let’s talk venue: Crystal Ballroom is EVERYTHING. Such a dynamic location, right in the heart of Fort Lauderdale beach. The staff was absolutely phenomenal to work with, especially the event coordinator: Mike.

No wedding can ever go perfectly as planned, things can get delayed, usually safety pins always save the day. Despite it all, everything went absolutely smoothly and it was a FUN wedding to remember.

Congrats again Alissa and Imran on your big day. Thank you for trusting us to capture the best day ever!
