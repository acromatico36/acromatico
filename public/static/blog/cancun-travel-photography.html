Cancun | Travel Photography

March 8, 2012

White sandy beaches, crystal clear turquoise waters,  scuba diving, snorkeling, LOTS of water sports, delicious Mexican cuisine, awesome nightlife and picturesque markets…We could only be talking about Cancun! Really really LOVED everything about this place specially the people, they are so friendly and everybody makes you feel welcome! We went to a tour that took us to Tulum, an ancient city in ruins and to Xel-Ha, a beautiful park full of wild birds and tropical fishes. We got to see the most amazing things underwater and go under caves! Here are some of our favorite shots from the trip. Enjoy!
