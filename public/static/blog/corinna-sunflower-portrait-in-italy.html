Corinna | Sunflower Portrait in Italy

July 15, 2017 • Italy

Flower power is the theme of this next session! We went with Corinna to a huge sunflower field in the outskirts of Milan. Had this all planned out that no matter rain or shine, we were going to hit up the field! Well…it kind of started raining and, as we got close, there was ZERO rain…and zero sun! The problem with sunflowers is just that, if the sun is out they’re happy and bloomed. If it’s hiding, they’re super sad and depressed! We decided to make the best out of the situation and accomplish our mission: shoot rain or shine! Despite the sun not being out, absolutely loved the skies on this day!
