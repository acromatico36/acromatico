Tiffany + Zach | Frenchman’s Creek Wedding

Photography • June 16, 2014

Tiffany and Zach had a very gorgeous, romantic wedding at the Frenchman’s Creek. Even though this beautiful wedding took only 6 weeks to plan, every detail was incredibly gorgeous from beginning to end. We had a blast with these two riding (and racing) in golf carts. Our beautiful bride not only found out the sex of the baby that she was carrying on her wedding day, but she also made a special gift for her groom… She revealed that they were having a baby girl on their big day! Later, they announced to the whole wedding guests the big news. What a touching wedding this was. We wish you both a very happy future and a lovely family!
