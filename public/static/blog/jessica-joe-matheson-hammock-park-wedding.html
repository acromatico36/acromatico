Jessica + Joe | Matheson Hammock Park Wedding

Photography • January 8, 2013

The second to last wedding of 2012…What can we say? We were extremely excited to shoot this wedding on the last weekend of the year! Jessica and Joe’s ceremony took place at the Matheson Hammock Park with the beautiful, breezy, beachy, sunny and sandy environment. They had their “first look” prior to their ceremony, and Joe could not keep his eyes off his stunning bride! We loved working with both their families, they were tons of fun. The reception took place at the Red Fish Grill, where the guests danced the night away to the breeze of the ocean. Jessica, Joe…we wish you guys the best!
