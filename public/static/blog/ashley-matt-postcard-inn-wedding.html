Ashley + Matt | Postcard Inn Wedding

Photography • November 24, 2014

This next wedding was truly adorable. Ashley and Matt had their first date in San Diego, ever since then they have been inseparable. They came down to Islamorada all the way from Texas and their family and friends followed them down from all over the country. It was so much fun shooting this laid back couple and all their amazing family and friends. Oh, and we almost forgot the most important part, their fur baby: Piper, she also walked the wedding;)
Their lovely ceremony and reception were held at the Post Card Inn in Islamorada, FL. What a great destination wedding location! We also enjoyed so much being able to fly in a water jet and shoot Ashley as she made her grand entrance. See for yourselves this super cool amazing wedding! Enjoy:
