Katia + Matteo | Italy after wedding session

Photography • June 26, 2012

And here’s the last Italy wedding post we’ll do for a while. It was SOOO hard to choose the pictures for this post! There are so many beautiful ones! We tried to narrow it down to some of our favorites. While taking the pictures we couldn’t believe how lucky we were to be shooting in Italy to a beautiful couple that were nothing but sweet, we love working with people like these two, so down to earth and friendly, they made our job so easy and fun! Hope you enjoy this session as much as we did. Katia e Matteo, a stato un piacere lavorare con voi, siete una coppia bellisima! Auguiri!
