Odalys + David | South Florida Engagement Session

Photography • October 14, 2010

It’s pretty often that we get couples asking to take pictures on our backyard as you can see on our blog. We were starting to wonder why, but after this session we fell in love again with our backyard and realized how awesome and how blessed we are to live in such a beautiful place. The day was ultra windy, but we managed to make the best out of it! First, we got Odalys and David wet on the pier (sorry Odalys!). Then we took them to the Haulover Marina Park where an awesome dude took us on his mega yacht, and we finished the day with a breathtaking sunset. We had so much fun with you guys and we can’t expect anything less for your wedding!
