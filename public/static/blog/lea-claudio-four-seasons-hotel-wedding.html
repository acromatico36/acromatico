Lea + Claudio | Four Seasons Hotel Wedding

July 11, 2011 • Miami, Coral Gables

A few months back we shot Lea and Claudio’s engagement in Vizcaya. They both flew in from Massachusetts, and a lot of folks came down from places all over the world to witness them join their lives together. Their day started with their getting ready pictures, followed by the ceremony at the Little Flower church in Coral Gables, and the wedding reception at the magnificent Four Seasons hotel in Miami. Everything was extremely beautiful, and we had hands down – one of the BEST vendor meals EVER!!!
