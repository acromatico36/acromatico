Lisa + Andrea | Hilton Ft. Lauderdale Wedding

Photography • April 17, 2014

Distance + love sometimes doesn’t equal to happiness. Lisa and Andrea overcame their long-distance battles and finally tied the knot to make it all end. You might remember these two from their lovely Flagler Museum engagement session. Their wedding wasn’t any different, these two were born to be in the spotlight. It was such a gorgeous day at the Fort Lauderdale Hilton, right on the beach, where their getting ready and wedding reception took place. Everything was so much fun and we had a pleasure to work with Events By Vento Designs that made everything look amazing!
