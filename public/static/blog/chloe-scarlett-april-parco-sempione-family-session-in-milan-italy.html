Chloe, Scarlett + April | Parco Sempione Family Session in Milan, Italy

July 5, 2017 • Italy

This next session was taken at the beautiful Parco Sempione in Milan, Italy. Even though there’s not too much “green” in Milan we found this cool spot with lots of greenery even beautiful hydrangeas, one of my faves! Summer time is a little hot and sticky in this city but these girls pulled it together and made all these photos seem effortless. Despite the heat and the serial killer mosquitoes, we survived! It was such a pleasure shooting this beautiful mommy and her two daughters. These are moments to always remember especially because you’re only a teen and a toddler once in your life.
