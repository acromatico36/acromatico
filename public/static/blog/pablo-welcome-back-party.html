Pablo | Welcome Back Party

Photography • December 27, 2010

This was an unforgettable party. It was a celebration of Pablo, who survived a major car accident and lived to tell the story. He was in a coma for several days, and his father promised that if his son survived, he would throw a mega party for all of those who were there to support him and his family. Well, he certainly came through with his promise, and held a huge party at CB Smith Park with lots of fun stuff and rides. We’re very happy to see you back on your feet and wish you the very best in life!
