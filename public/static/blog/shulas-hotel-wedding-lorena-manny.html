Shula's Hotel Wedding | Lorena + Manny

August 28, 2020 • Miami

Lorena and Many where determined to make this wedding happen, no matter what! Aside from the pandemic, that same day we where supposed to be hit by a category 1 hurricane BUT it didn’t happen! We had the most beautiful blue skies and were even able to fly a drone to take cool pictures and videos of the party!

The day started at the Sula’s Hotel in Miami where Lorena was getting ready with our beautiful and talented friend “Jany did my hair” check her out! She is THE BEST hairstylist in Miami hands down and now she is exploring more of her art and adding make up to the table!

After Lorena got gorgeously ready we we headed to the ceremony where Manny got to see his future wife for the first time. We cannot begin to describe the how jaw dropped what Many when he saw her. It was a beautiful moment to witness.

The night finished with the beautiful reception they planned at the Shula’s Hotel, everything was so detailed and turned out so amazing and we where truly impressed how everyone followed the new guidelines for Covid 19. so for all of our brides thinking if its doable to do a wedding during these times, it truly is! you just got to get creative and improvise like Lorena and Many did!
