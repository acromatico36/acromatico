ashley + brad | university of miami engagement session

Photography • July 29, 2011

We were heading to UM  and a HUGE storm caught us on the way. We were so worried that this was going to ruin our session, but luckily we live in south Florida! In one second you can have the perfect storm, and the next you’re enjoying the most beautiful day at the beach. As soon as we arrived the storm cleared up and we got the perfect day to shoot! Ashley and Brad both flew from Chicago to their hometown, Miami to take their engagement pictures. They met at the University of Miami and it was very special for them having their pictures taken here while remembering all of the things they did together. We had so much fun with them! Can’t wait to their wedding day!
