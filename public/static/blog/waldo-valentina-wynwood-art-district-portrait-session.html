Waldo + Valentina | Wynwood Art District Portrait Session

Photography • January 11, 2013

Winter-in-New-York, graffiti walls? Nope, just another session at the Wynwood Art District! This time no engagement, no wedding coming up, not really a special occasion…it was a just-for-fun, brother/sister (and mom) portrait session. They couldn’t have picked a better place for an International DJ and soon-to-be Forever 21 model, lol! Being our cousins we felt right at home with them and we all ended up having a blast.
