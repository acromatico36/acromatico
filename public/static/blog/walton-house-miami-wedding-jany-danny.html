Walton House Miami Wedding | Jany + Danny

Photography • August 25, 2017

We had the pleasure of photographing this beautiful Walton House Miami wedding after-session for our our very dear friends Jany and Danny. They chose this amazing little gem for their after wedding session. It’s been a little over a year since their wedding day but they looked as radiant as they did when they got married. We ran into some little friends while shooting and absolutely fell in love with a horse from this place. I don’t know about you guys but there is just something so romantic about horses and brides that we JUST love!
