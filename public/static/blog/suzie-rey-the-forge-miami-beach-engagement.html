Suzie + Rey | The Forge Miami Beach Engagement

September 29, 2012 • Miami, South Florida, Italy

If you live in South Florida, during this time of the year, you know these two words when you hear them: MIAMI SPICE. What does that have to do with this session?  We got to shoot nonetheless in one of their featured restaurant: The Forge. Time to bring it back to the pearls and the vests, Suzie and Rey definitely put on their “fancy shoes” for this one. They were almost as excited as we were to shoot some pictures in their historic v.i.p. wine cellar. You can only go in there on a private tour, Rey pulled some strings and managed to get us all in there, needless to say we loved it. Brought our senses back to Chianti, Italy.
