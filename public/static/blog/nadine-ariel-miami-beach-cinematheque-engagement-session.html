nadine + ariel | miami beach cinematheque engagement session

October 25, 2011 • Miami

Today’s engagement session was really unique. It’s the first time we do an actual session in a movie theater, and this is no ordinary one! The Miami Beach Cinematheque is an artsy upscale theater, where they show only independent films, highly recommended by these two movie lovers.  We’re very happy with the outcome of the session, Nadine and Ariel made our day lots of fun and they’re really great people with loving personalities.  We love it when clients take us to new places never discovered, it makes our job very exciting!

Working our butts off…that’s how we roll!
