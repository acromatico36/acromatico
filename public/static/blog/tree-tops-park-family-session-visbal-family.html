Tree Tops Park Family Session | Visbal Family

Photography • December 3, 2019

Every time Ximena writes to schedule their yearly family photo session you cannot understand the excitement here at the office! Not only because we feel like we are part of the family already having photographed them since baby number one and we love them so much! but also because we know we are going to see Benny, the cutest dog EVER! Seriously guys, this dog is out of this world! Maybe because he is a therapy dog but we’ve never seen any that behaves this way, so chill and calm and loving.

Enjoy this gorgeous family and if you are thinking you would like a park session, check this one out.. Tree Tops Park Family Sessions always come out dreamy!
