Tica + Justin | Douglas Entrance Coral Gables Wedding

October 24, 2013 • Coral Gables

Our beautiful sister (and cool assistant) got married last week with the man of her dreams at the Douglas Entrance in Coral Gables.. And we’re truly excited to introduce a new addition to the team with our amazingly talented destination wedding photographer Victor Zerga.  Words can’t explain how thrilled we are for such beautiful memories.  We have always admired Victor’s work and it’s a true honor to have him as our newest addition to our amazing team, soon to be featured on our upcoming new website.  Congratulations Tica & Justin, we love you guys so much!
