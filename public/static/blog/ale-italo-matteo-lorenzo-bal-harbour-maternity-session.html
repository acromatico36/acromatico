Ale, Italo, Matteo & Lorenzo | Bal Harbour Maternity Session

Photography • September 19, 2016

Its not everyday we get to take photos of ourselves so we decided to make this post special as our second baby boy is coming soon! Bal Harbour will always hold a special place in our hearts, as its where we began our journey as a married couple and looking back so many nice memories were built.  Big special thanks to our in house photographers Tica and Vanessa, you girls continue to surprise us and we couldn’t be happier with the beautiful memories we have to keep.  We also tried something completely different and our sister Tica was all up to the challenge, taking beautiful photos in the middle of the sea, we started at the Haulover sandbar and ended in a beautiful island nearby.  Miami looks best in the water, and we are so thankful and blessed to have our boys enjoy the little boat ride and photos.  Here are some of our favorite ones!
