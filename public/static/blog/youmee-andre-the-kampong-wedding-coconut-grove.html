Youmee + Andre | The Kampong Wedding Coconut Grove

Photography • March 21, 2014

It’s not every day people from opposite sides of the world meet, fall in love, and get married. This Kampong Wedding was one of a kind. With the two different worlds of Korea and Brazil attracting, you have the beautiful Youmee and stunning Andrè getting married. We truly enjoyed this wedding and how laid back everyone was. No matter the language, nor the culture, love always finds a way. These two are crazy for eachother, it was just so evident by the way they looked at one another. Korea and Brazil can definitely party! Enjoy some of our faves from this super awesome wedding in Coconut Grove.
