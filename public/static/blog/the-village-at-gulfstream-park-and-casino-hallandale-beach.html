The village at Gulfstream Park and Casino | Hallandale Beach

September 1, 2011

This post is a little bit different from what you’ve normally seen in the past years. We received a call to take pictures of a few stores at the Gulfstream Park in Hallandale Beach. The developer, JCI Development Corp, wanted to showcase their work for their latest projects, which feature the all time famous high end electronic store Bang & Olufsen and fashion stores Claudio Milano. We’re very impressed with their high quality work and how fast they built these stores. Enjoy the post!
