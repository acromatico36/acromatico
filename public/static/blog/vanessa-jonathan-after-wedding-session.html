Vanessa + Jonathan | After Wedding Session

Photography • May 19, 2010

We had the pleasure of shooting Vanessa and Jonathan’s after wedding session in downtown Miami. Both being true artists, they were very easy to lead creativity and we had tons of fun shooting their pictures in Miami’s suburb streets. We hit Bayside afterwards, which is Miami’s most turistic shopping plaza with live music and great food. Thanks for such a wonderful time!
