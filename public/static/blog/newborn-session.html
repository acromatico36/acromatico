Newborn Session | Baby Noah

Photography • March 28, 2023

There’s nothing quite like the arrival of a new baby to fill a family with joy and excitement. And when that baby belongs to your best friend and the one that you consider a sister, and your brother, the feeling is indescribable.

I am thrilled to introduce you to the newest addition to our family, baby Noah. He was born just a few weeks ago but has already captured our hearts and filled our lives with so much love and happiness.

As an auntie and uncle, my husband and I feel so blessed to be a part of Noah’s life in such a special way by capturing all his special moments as he grows, starting with this newborn session.

We had the pleasure of doing not only a newborn session with baby Noah, but also got to spend some time with Diego and Carol while on the delivery room and took advantage to get some memories for them. 

For us, capturing these photos of baby Noah is not just about creating beautiful images to hang on our walls or share here. It’s about celebrating his life and the love that brought him into the world. It’s about cherishing every moment we have with him and creating memories that will last a lifetime.

If you’re expecting a new arrival in your family, we can’t recommend enough the importance of capturing these precious moments with professional newborn photography. Not only will you have beautiful images to treasure forever, but you’ll also have the opportunity to celebrate the love and joy that your new baby brings to your life <3
