leonor + sergio | wynwood art district engagement session

January 24, 2012 • Miami, Wynwood

Downtown Miami is always full of surprises, and this session taught us that doing extensive research can find you lots of artsy treasures, beautiful sceneries and amazing nightlife.  All the props go to Sergio and Leonor for making this happen, we love their enthusiasm, attention to detail and the awesome locations they chose.  First off, we started in Margaret Pace Park, where they had a beautiful picnic all set up!  Then we moved to the all-time amazing walls of Wynwood Art District, where we found some cool couches and paintings, and a special thanks goes to Caffeina Lounge in Wynwood for letting us use their facility to finish our day.  Can’t wait to their wedding, you guys look so happy together, thank you for making us part of your day, can’t wait to witness your I do’s!
