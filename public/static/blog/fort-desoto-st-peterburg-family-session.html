Fort De Soto St. Petersburg Family Session

Photography • December 5, 2018

We first met Brittany and Travis years ago when we had the pleasure of capturing their Wedding here in South Florida. They moved up north and a few months ago we had a surprise email from Brittany telling us they now have two kids and would love to do a family session at Fort De Soto in St. Petersburg. For us it was a no-brainer! We enjoy traveling so much and we always LOVE it when our couples call us back years later to keep on covering their special moments in life.  It feels amazing to witness how the family grows and the bond and connection they have crated throughout the years. Brittany and Travis, you guys have created and amazing little family and we are beyond happy we got yo share this special time with both of you again! Enjoy some of our favorites from this beautiful Fort De Soto St. Petersburg Family Session. Even though it was extremely windy and cold for sunny South Florida standards, they where troopers and made it a breeze (no pun intended) to capture these beautiful images…
