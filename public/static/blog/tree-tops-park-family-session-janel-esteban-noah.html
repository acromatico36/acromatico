Tree Tops Park Family Session | Janel, Esteban & Noah

May 8, 2018 • Davie

We’ve been photographing Janel & Esteban since their engagement session back in 2010. We’ve shot their wedding, after wedding even an underwater session. We’re so excited for a very overdue session but this time with the whole family: 8 years later and with the cutest little man, Noah!

Noah is now over a year old so they decided to come down from Orlando to do their family session at the Tree Tops Park in Davie. They’re hard core fans of Barça so they had to represent! It was so much fun shooting these cuties. The best part was getting little Noah to laugh, especially with his grandpa! This is definitely one of our favorite stages in the toddler life.

Check out some of our dear friends cute family session. We love you guys so much, thank you for coming down to see us and letting us capture your beautiful family!
