amanda + laurent | coral gables day after session

July 26, 2011 • Coral Gables, Coconut Grove

We had a great time photographing Amanda and Laurent literally the next day after their wedding, they were such great sports! We started at the Mayfair Hotel in Coconut Grove (super awesome hotel!) and ended the day at the Matheson Hammock Park in Coral Gables. We love when we meet people like these two, so real with such a great connection with themselves, it just makes our job so easy!
