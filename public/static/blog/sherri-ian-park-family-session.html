Sherri + Ian | Park Family Session

Photography • February 18, 2015

Wedding, maternity, newborn session, baby session, now back to a maternity/family session. We have literally been through everything with Sherri and Ian! Yes, you heard right, another maternity session for this gorgeous mommy-to-be for the second time! Over the last years, we’ve worked with this beautiful family in all of the biggest events of their lives. We couldn’t be happier when she called us to shoot their pictures once again. Sherri and Ian are expecting a baby girl. Little Michael promises to love her, as he already loves his mommy’s belly. Check out this awesome little family’s session at the Esplanade Park in Fort Lauderdale. We can’t wait to meet the new baby girl, congrats again guys!
