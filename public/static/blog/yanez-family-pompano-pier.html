Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a family portrait session at Pompano Beach Pier cost?

Family portrait sessions start at $595 for 90 minutes of coverage at Pompano Beach Pier or other South Florida beach locations. This includes professional editing, an online gallery, and high-resolution digital downloads. Most families receive 60-100 beautifully edited images that tell your family's story.

When is the best time for beach family photos at Pompano Pier?

Golden hour—the hour before sunset—creates the most magical lighting for beach family portraits. At Pompano Beach Pier, this typically occurs between 6:00-7:30 PM depending on the season. The warm, soft light is incredibly flattering and creates that dreamy, timeless quality in your images. We also offer sunrise sessions for families who prefer softer, more private beach conditions.

What should we wear for beach family photos?

Choose light, flowy fabrics in coordinating colors rather than matching outfits. Soft blues, whites, creams, and pastels photograph beautifully against the ocean backdrop. Avoid heavy patterns, logos, and neon colors. Barefoot on the beach always looks natural and elegant. We provide a detailed style guide with your session booking to help you create cohesive, timeless looks.

Do you photograph large families or just immediate family?

We love photographing families of all sizes! Whether it's just parents and children, or a multi-generational gathering with grandparents, aunts, uncles, and cousins, we capture both large group portraits and intimate family combinations. Extended family sessions allow extra time to ensure everyone gets beautiful images together and individually.

What makes Pompano Beach Pier ideal for family portraits?

Pompano Beach Pier offers the perfect combination of iconic architecture, beautiful ocean views, and accessible beach areas. The pier's wooden structure provides interesting leading lines and depth, while the sandy beach allows for natural, candid moments. It's less crowded than some South Florida beaches, giving us privacy for your session while still offering that classic Florida coastal aesthetic.

How do you work with children during family sessions?

We specialize in working with children of all ages! Our approach is playful, patient, and pressure-free. We encourage movement, play, and authentic interaction rather than forcing stiff poses. Kids respond beautifully to our documentary-style approach, which captures their genuine personalities. We also work quickly and efficiently, keeping sessions fun and stress-free for everyone.
