Brittany + Travis | Cardinal Gibbons High School Engagement Session

April 11, 2012

We LOVE to see when two people are perfect for each other. And that was the case of Brittany and Travis! What an awesome morning we had with them! We went to take pictures exactly where everything started, their high school. They’ve been together for 8 years now and look so happy! We started the session on the baseball field were they used to play and ended on the hallway in front of their classroom. We had so much fun! Can’t wait to their wedding!
