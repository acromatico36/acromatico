Janel + Esteban | Bill Baggs After Wedding Session

Photography • February 12, 2011

You may remember Janel and Esteban’s wedding from last year. This is the first time we take pictures at the Bill Baggs State Park in Key Biscayne. What was amazing was not just the spectacular views of the ocean, the amazing sunset or the beautiful lighthouse set at the beach. But rather the fact these two are just so photogenic and the connection they had with each other just made this after session a really easy and fun experience. Our only regret is that we wish we had more time to take more pictures! Enjoy the set 🙂
