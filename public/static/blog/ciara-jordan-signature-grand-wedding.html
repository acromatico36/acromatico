Ciara + Jordan | Signature Grand Wedding

Photography • March 5, 2013

This wasn’t just any wedding, it was one of our dearest family member with her new hubby. Ciara and Jordan mean the world to us and when we found out we were shooting their wedding we were extremely honored. We’ve known Ciara for many many years, and even though we met Jordan a couple of years ago, it feels like we’ve known him forever too! They make the perfect match and we couldn’t be happier for them. Their wedding was held at the gorgeous Signature Grand and it was not only beautiful but very sentimental for all of us. We loved capturing every single moment, from Daddy’s first look to Jordan’s father playing a special song with his harmonica for them. Ciara. . .Jordan . . . We LOVE you guys! Congrats and enjoy your awesome honeymoon. We hope you guys enjoy some of our favorite shots for the night, it was really hard choosing!
