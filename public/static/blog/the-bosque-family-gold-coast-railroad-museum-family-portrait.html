the bosque family | gold coast railroad museum family portrait

January 27, 2012

What a treat was to shoot this beautiful family! this was the first time we do a family session, a bit different from the engagement and wedding work but SO MUCH FUN!!  Claudia and Felix will be celebrating next month their 20th anniversary! And decided to do a retro themed session mixed with some park-beach action! We started the day at the Gold Coast Railroad Museum and then headed to the Matheson Hammock Park. We realized the we definitely love working with kids, specially when they are so nice, funny and charismatic like Brandon and Danny. We also loved the awesome dynamic and connection this family has. Congratulations Claudia and Felix for the beautiful marriage and family you two have! Have a happy 20th anniversary!!!
