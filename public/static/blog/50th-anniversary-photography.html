How much does an anniversary photo session cost?

Anniversary photo sessions start at $450 for 60-90 minutes of coverage. This includes professional editing, online gallery with high-resolution downloads, and typically 50-70 final images.

What's the best time for anniversary photos?

Golden hour is ideal for anniversary sessions in South Florida—typically 1-1.5 hours before sunset. This timing provides soft, warm light that's flattering and romantic.

What should we wear for anniversary photos?

Choose outfits that reflect your personal style and coordinate beautifully. Many couples opt for semi-formal to formal attire—flowing dresses, suits, or even recreating your wedding day look!

Can we include family in our anniversary session?

Absolutely! Many couples include children, grandchildren, or extended family. We typically start with couple portraits, then bring in family for group shots.

What are the best locations for anniversary photos?

Top locations include meaningful spots—where you got engaged, where you married, or favorite places you've visited together. In South Florida, popular choices include Vizcaya, parks, and beaches.
