Stephanie + Danny | Hugh Taylor Birch Park Engagement

October 12, 2012

Everybody knows who Allie and Noah are, especially when we start talking about the Notebook. What does that have to do with today’s blog? Two words: Notebook Engagement! Stephanie and Danny picked this very romantic, just-like-the-movies, theme. When they told us, we were thrilled since it’s one of our favorite movies. Some  people find it horrible when it rains on picture day, for us, we’re glad it did…it gave the scene the last touch! This particular session was very risky, we had to jump in a canoe with them…but don’t worry, the camera’s made it safe lol.
