Melissa + Spencer | The Addison Boca Raton Wedding

Photography • November 15, 2012

We shot the Wades! Well, not exactly from the Miami Heat …more like Melissa and Spencer: “The Wades”. Melissa and her girls got ready at the Boca Beach Club in Boca Raton, and she took a very special guest with her…Little Dolce, her puppy! He was ready in his tux dressed like a little groom, so cute! They were doing the “first look” at the Boca Beach Club, but Melissa wasn’t too sure she really wanted to do it, so she teased him Spencer a little longer and he had to wait for his “first look” but nothing could stop this groom-to-be from seeing his bride for the first time. Spencer walked blindfolded toward his beautiful bride-to-be, as the whole crowd awed at them. Everything turned out beautifully! Melissa was more gorgeous than ever, and their wedding was a blast.
