Mondestin Family | Maternity Session

Photography • November 1, 2012

We schedule the session at the Matheson Hammock Park but unfortunately Sandy flooded the entire bay area but it worked out pretty well because of the beautiful sunny day and green backgrounds at the park! We loved working with the Mondestin family and are very excited to be showing you a few of our favorites!
