Wedding

tiffany + brendan | delray beach club wedding

July 27, 2011

Ahhh perfect wedding days…How much we love them!!! Tiffany has to be the most detail orientated bride we have ever gotten, this wedding was perfection from beginning to end.  We started the day at the Seagate Hotel in Delray where we took Tiffany’s getting ready pictures, following the first look pictures at the Delray beach club. Everyone flew in from Utah and traveled from other places in the world to witness their beautiful destination wedding. You know that everybody loves the bride and groom when you have more than 8 toasts in a row! And we absolutely loved the romantic meal we got in a private dining restaurant with a personal singer and delicious food!! Many thanks to the bride, groom and family for making this experience so thoughtful and fun for us.
