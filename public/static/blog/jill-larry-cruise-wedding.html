Jill + Larry | Cruise Wedding

January 9, 2017 • Fort Lauderdale

This is it, the LAST wedding of 2016! It was our honor shooting this beautiful wedding that took place on a boat. Jill and Larry said their “I do’s” days before the year was over. The weather was perfect for cruising the Intracoastal in Fort Lauderdale. With the great sunset and their families and friends before them, these two lovebirds tied the knot. Enjoy our last wedding of the year. We look forward to this 2017 with many more adventures to come. Stay tuned! Congrats again guys on your big day!
