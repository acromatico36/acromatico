Silvia + Max | Longan's Place Wedding

April 29, 2015

It was our first time in Longan’s place, but we fell in love with it right away. Silvia and Max chose this gorgeous location for their beautiful bohemian wedding. From the romantic gardens, the relaxing waterfall to the night lighting candles, this wedding was just perfect! Despite the couple of drops that fell down, the skies cleared up when it was time for this beautiful bride to walk down the aisle. With their families and friends, they said their “I do’s”.
