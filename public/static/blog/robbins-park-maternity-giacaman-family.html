Robbins Park Maternity | Giacaman Family

May 14, 2021

By far this is one of the most special sessions this year! Not only this is our beautiful sister and brother welcoming another baby to the happy family of 3, but this happens to be the very awaited maternity session for THE FIRST baby girl of the family! We are so extremely excited to meet baby Gabriela coming soon!

This session seemed it was not going to happen yesterday, but despite the 80% chances of rain and the fact that it did rain and the park we had planed closed for the day, we were determined to make it happen..and we did!! So happy we didn’t backed out because the day turned out to be beautiful and the location we found last minute exceeded our expectations. We ended up going to Robbins Park in Davie and it was a home run. this place is full of green open areas, barn style fences and even cute donkeys.. Perfect for a country looking maternity session.
