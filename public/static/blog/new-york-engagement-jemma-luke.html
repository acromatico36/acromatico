New York Engagement | Jemma + Luke

March 6, 2019 • New York

Ireland meets America! Check out this engagement session in New York.

I don’t know what we’re more obsessed with: Irish accents or this cute couple right here! Jemma and Luke are great friends of ours. As soon as we found out he popped the question, I just couldn’t wait to shoot their engagement session in Warwick, New York.

It was forecasted to be rainy-ish and snow? Thankfully, none of the two happened! The day couldn’t have been more perfect for these lovebirds. We winged this sesh and literally planned it overnight. What I love about Jemma and Luke is how spontaneous they are! All they said was where and when and they were just there. No questions asked! This totally YOLO ( I don’t even think we use this anymore but I’m bringing it back in 2019 ) engagement session was a total freeze fest. From the pictures, the day looks perfectly warm with a little breeze. Welp…that’s not exactly how it went! These guys (especially Jemma) were serious beasts taking in the cold while looking their bests. I think I almost lost a toe, and we definitely were so close to frost bite LOL. Despite the non-deer-tick we ran into, the cold, the hunger, these guys killed it! I seriously fell in love with them even more, realizing just how perfect of a match they are.
