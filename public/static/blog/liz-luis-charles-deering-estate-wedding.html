Liz + Luis | Charles Deering Estate Wedding

April 30, 2012 • Miami, Coral Gables

This was a beautiful wedding from beginning to end. We had, once again,  a blast working with Liz and Luis, you might remember them from their dynamic engagement session at the Miami Fair . We started the day at the Hyatt Hotel in Coral Gables where they got ready for their big day. Then we headed to the beautiful Charles Deering Estate park in Coral Gables where the ceremony and reception were held. We hope you enjoy reliving these moments just as we did capturing them.
