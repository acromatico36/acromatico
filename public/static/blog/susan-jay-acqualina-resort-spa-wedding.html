Susan + Jay | Acqualina Resort & Spa Wedding

July 11, 2014 • Miami, South Florida

With only 4 months to plan their wedding, Susan and Jay chose South Florida for their destination wedding. Coming down all the way from  Virginia Beach, this Italian and Jewish couple had two traditional ceremonies to have a little bit of both worlds. Their beautiful, intimate wedding reception, took place at the Acqualina Resort & Spa. Not only did it have some gorgeous, breathtaking views, but also, the decorating team did an outstanding job on the gorgeous, well-detailed, reception arrangements.
