Tree Tops Park Maternity | Josey + Freddie

October 8, 2021

There’s nothing better than watching a family grow, this time, with T W I N S. I had the honor of shooting Josey and Freddie’s Maternity sesh at Tree Tops Park for a true evergreen experience.

We just love Tree Tops Park and it never gets old coming here. It’s perfect for all types of sessions but this kind was truly something else.

They weren’t planning on having covid babies, little did they expect they’d be having covid twins! Yet, they rolled with it. Josey is a true rockstar for carrying these two little ones like a true queen.

We had a great time shooting their maternity session and despite a few setbacks they managed to make my job so easy and to effortlessly look amazing.

Congrats guys on your new adventure, we can’t wait to meet your little boy and girl this arriving this December!
