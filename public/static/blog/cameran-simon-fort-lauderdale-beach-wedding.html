Cameran + Simon | Fort Lauderdale Beach Wedding

May 11, 2011 • Fort Lauderdale, South Florida

Our biggest wedding post EVER! Over 130 pictures and countless smiles. And talk about destination wedding! Cameran and Simon flew all the way from California to get married in South Florida, while the rest of their global family flew as far as from London! It was the most relaxing, laid back, emotional and sentimental wedding we have attended to so far. These English / American folks had the best bride and groom dedications we’ve ever heard in a wedding – period. We’re so happy for these two, they look so great together! Enjoy the set, more pictures coming soon to our print store!
