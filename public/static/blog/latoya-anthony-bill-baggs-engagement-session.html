Latoya + Anthony | Bill Baggs Engagement Session

Photography • July 15, 2011

This is the first time we are clients of our very own client! Latoya is part of our local and awesome Citibank branch and Anthony is a huge Miami heat fan. We took the pictures on the final game, really hoping to win the champion cup. We all know we didn’t but we’re still loyal fans. We had a great time taking their engagement pictures in Key Biscayne’s Bill Baggs park and ended our session at a spectacular view in Miami’s beautiful skyline. Can’t wait to next month’s wedding!!
