Coral Gables Entrance Park: A Photographic Tour

Coral Gables Entrance Park is one of the most popular tourist destinations in the city. The park is known for its beautiful gardens, diverse wildlife, and stunning views of the cityscape. If you’re looking for a place to take some professional photos, this is the perfect spot!

We’ve been professional photographers for years, and have had the opportunity to photograph many of the city’s most popular attractions. But there’s something about Coral Gables Entrance Park that always leaves us feeling inspired.

Whether you’re a professional photographer or just someone who enjoys taking pictures, we hope you’ll enjoy this photographic tour of the park!

A beautiful place to relax and take in the scenery

As you enter the park, you’ll instantly be surrounded by beautiful gardens and lush vegetation.

These gorgeous Bugambilia flowers, European-style pergola, and bright blue skies make the portraits artistic and come to life.

We also love the water pond and the reflection it makes on the photos, with the serenity vibes it gives.
