Khadine + Dwayne | Miami Wedding

October 8, 2010 • Miami

We feel so fortunate to have such an awesome job, where we get to meet awesome couples, eat awesome food, dance awesome music and have good times. This time was not an exception. Khadine and Dwayne had one of the most fun wedding we’ve been to so far. Their ceremony and reception was held on north Miami beach at the Newport Beachside hotel. It was a very intimate wedding, with lots of emotions and tons of dancing! And WOW, these people can dance!!!
